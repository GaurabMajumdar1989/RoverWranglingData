Magic is not a hidden art but a rhythmic pulse felt by every living creature, flowing through the roots of the silver-leafed Eldertrees that hum in harmony with the wind.
The peace of this realm is guarded by the Winged Sentinels, warriors who ride majestic gryphons through the shifting clouds of the Upper Reach.
